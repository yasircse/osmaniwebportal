About Us

OSMANI is one of the leading Consulting Company with standing of more than 53 years in the Consulting Profession. Established in 1960, has grown from a small private group of Engineers & Architects to a large organization in the shape of Private Limited Company in 1971. It has at its credit 4500+ prestigious projects of National & International importance spread all over Pakistan and abroad. The Clientele of Osmani includes Governments, Corporations, Private Enterprises and agencies like WORLD BANK, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK, UNDP, UNCHS (Habitat), ABU DHABI MUNICIPALITY, etc. OSMANI’s mission is to deliver efficient and economic technological services & solutions in Engineering, Architecture, Planning and Technology. Since last 53 years OSMANI is providing the innovative Technology advanced services to its clients in the fields of Engineering, Architecture, Planning, Master Planning, Infrastructure Planning & Designing and Information Technology alongwith the detailed supervision and project management.

Chairman

The Chairman of the company, Engr. I. A. Osmani, has been a pioneer in the field of consulting Engineering in Pakistan and is one of the leading figures of Pakistan’s Engineering Community and is currently the Senior Vice Chairman of Pakistan Engineering Council, the apex body to regulate Engineering profession and Practice. Under the chairman’s dynamic leadership and professional excellence, Osmani has successfully completed many major project of National & International importance in Pakistan & abroad.
